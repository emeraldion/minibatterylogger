The Statistics View

The Statistics View contains a list of battery “snapshots”, that is, a list of records of the maximum capacity and cycle count ordered by date.

Snapshots are plotted to the chart in the upper half of the window. The increasing blue graph plots the cycle count versus time, while the decreasing red graph plots the max capacity versus time. Additionally, a dotted orange line represents the regression line of the capacity. It is a linear approximation of the capacity graph.

Snapshots are automatically saved when a property of your battery has changed, so you don’t have to bother saving them by hand. Snapshots can always be added and removed manually.

You can add a custom note to a snapshot, e.g. “Initial value” or “Reached 100th cycle”.

The chart can be copied to the pasteboard or dragged as an image.

To add a comment to a snapshot, double click on the table cell.

To add or remove a snapshot, use the paired buttons on the bottom of the window.
