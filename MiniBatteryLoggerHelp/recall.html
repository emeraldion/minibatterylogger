Battery Recall Assistant

The Battery Recall Assistant helps you to determine if your battery is eligible for a battery replacement program by Apple.

The Assistant asks you the battery serial number, the computer serial number (it is already filled in for your convenience) and your country. Then verifies if your battery is eligible for the program

WARNING: the Battery Recall Assistant is provided as a convenience only. You should not rely blindly on it, because Apple may change the rules for the recall programs at any time. It is recommended that you check the Apple Support website by clicking on the More info… button to get the latest updates.

To verify if your battery is eligible for the recall program, enter the battery serial number, the computer serial number and choose your country, then press Check.

To learn your battery serial number, power off the laptop, then disconnect the AC cable, extract the battery and flip it to read the label (see picture).
