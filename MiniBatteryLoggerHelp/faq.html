Frequently Asked Questions

If you still have a question about MiniBatteryLogger, you’re likely to find the answer here below.

I’m trying to get shared battery data without success

Be sure you’re connected to Internet. If anything else fails, the web service could be under maintenance. Please try again later.

What informations are being sent when I share my battery data?

Your privacy is always on top of the priority list. All data is sent anonymously. The only sensible information used to let you update your previous data is a hash calculated as the MD5 sum of your computer’s serial number. MD5 hashes are notoriously hard (not impossible, though) to decipher.

I have a Pismo and MiniBatteryLogger doesn’t recognize my battery

Although MiniBatteryLogger is ready for multiple battery systems, this feature is not currently implemented. If a consistent number of Pismo users should request this feature, I’d be glad to add it.
