The Chart View

The Chart View shows the graph of the charge (in percentage, vertical axis) and of the amperage (Amperes) versus time (horizontal axis).

Relevant power events are marked over the graphs with a bubble. You can customize the graph colors and enable or disable the bubbles or the amperage graph from the Preferences.

The chart can be copied to the pasteboard and dragged as an image. Hold the option (‚å•) key while dragging.

To tighten/broaden the time interval visualized you can use the coupled toolbar icons plus and minus, or the menu items Zoom In and Zoom Out under the View menu.

To fit the entire graph in the chart view, use the toolbar icons with the diamond, or the menu item Zoom to fit under the View menu.

To pan over the chart, use the coupled toolbar icons left and right, or the menu items Shift left and Shift right under the View menu.

The Session Inspector lets you inspect all the events in the session. Click or drag the mouse button on the chart view to select events of the visualized session. You will read the event details in the inspector panel.

To show the Session Inspector, double click on the chart view, or use the toolbar button Inspect, or the menu item Session Inspector under the Session menu.

The Sessions Browser is a drawer containing the list of the previously saved monitoring sessions.

Click on a list item to load and visualize the session in the chart view. Use the buttons in the bottom of the drawer to quickly launch actions on the selected session.

You can save a small comment for every session double clicking on the table cell.

Sessions can be dragged and copied to the pasteboard as CSV data.
