The Comparison View

The Comparison View is a great source of information that helps you to compare your battery performance to the batteries of other users.

In the upper part of the window there are four columns of statistics. The three columns on the right are, respectively, the best, average and worst item in the shared archive. The leftmost columns are your laptop’s ones. When they are better than the average, they are displayed in green. When worse, they are displayed in red.

If you’re connected to Internet, MiniBatteryLogger will automatically retrieve information about other batteries of your computer model.

MiniBatteryLogger will try hard to guess your computer model. You can always manually correct the computer model if it is not right. The battery purchase date will also help the shared data tracking system to elaborate battery wear trends. Please don’t cheat! ;)

Collected data are publicly and anonymously available at the Shared Battery Data Archive in the form of a web service.

To update the shared battery data, use the Get shared data button, or the menu item of the same name under the Battery menu.

To share your battery data, use the Share your data button, or the menu item of the same name under the Battery menu.
