Register your Copy

MiniBatteryLogger is released as shareware software. If you’re using it regularly, you should register a license.

The unregistered version can be evaluated without restrictions for a limited period of time. After the evaluation period has expired, it will prompt the user for registration until it is registered.

Follow this link to register your copy of MiniBatteryLogger.

To enter your registration codes, choose Enter Registration Key from MiniBatteryLogger menu and then choose Registration.

Your registration codes are sent to you by email after registering.
